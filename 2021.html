VIDEOGRAPHY DOMAIN

PHOTOGRAPHY DOMAIN

  • For Photography the theme is YOUR BEST SHOT.
  • Photo Manipulation is not allowed.
  • Some Basic Editing is allowed.
  • Participants have to send both original and Edited one
  • The Size of Photo should be greater than 3MB.

ART DOMAIN
